ABOUT US

Rosalia's Kitchen was established in 1989 with the goal to provide the best dining experience at cheaper rates in the heart of the city. Rosalia's kitchen offers a wide palate selection of italian cuisine. We also have a young chef's programme to encourage the pursuit of an interest and talent one might possess in the kitchen.
